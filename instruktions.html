How to Play "Rock, Paper, Scissors, Lizard, Spock"

  1. Objective:
    • The goal of the game is to choose an option (Rock, Paper, Scissors, Lizard, or Spock) that beats your opponent's choice. You'll be playing against the computer.
  2. Choices:
    3. You have five choices:
    • Rock crushes Scissors and Lizard
    • Paper covers Rock and disproves Spock
    • Scissors cuts Paper and decapitates Lizard
    • Lizard eats Paper and poisons Spock
    • Spock smashes Scissors and vaporizes Rock
  3. How to Play:
    • Click one of the five buttons (Rock, Paper, Scissors, Lizard, or Spock) to make your choice.
    • The computer will then randomly select its choice.
    • The result of the round will be displayed:
    • If you win, your score will increase.
    • If you lose, the computer's score will increase.
    • If it's a tie, no points are awarded.
  4. Levels:
    • The game starts at Level 1. As you win rounds, you will progress to higher levels.
      • '
    • The higher the level, the faster you must make your decision, and the computer may get more difficultto beat
  5. Winning & Losing:
    • After each round, you will see the result (Win, Lose or Tie)
    • Continue playing and try to reachthe highest level possible by winning more rounds.
  6. Sounds:
    • Sound effects will play when you click a button, win, lose, or tie a round.
  7. Resetting the Game:
    • You can reset the game at any time by clicking the "Reset Game" button, which will restart your scores and level back to 1.
