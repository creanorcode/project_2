How to Play "Rock, Paper, Scissors, Lizard, Spock"

  1. Objective:
    • The goal of the game is to choose an option (Rock, Paper, Scissors, Lizard, or Spock) that beats the computer's choice. The game ends when the computer reaches 7 points, and the computer wins the game.

  2. Choices:
    3. You have five options to choose from:
    • Rock crushes Scissors and Lizard
    • Paper covers Rock and disproves Spock
    • Scissors cuts Paper and decapitates Lizard
    • Lizard eats Paper and poisons Spock
    • Spock smashes Scissors and vaporizes Rock

  3. How to Play:
    • Click one of the five buttons (Rock, Paper, Scissors, Lizard, or Spock) to make your choice.
    • The computer will then randomly select its choice.
    • The result of the round will be displayed:
      • If you win, your score will increase.
      • If you lose, the computer's score will increase.
      • If it's a tie, no points are awarded.

  4. Levels:
    • The game starts at Level 1. As you win rounds, the game become progressively more difficult.
    • As your score increases, you will move to higher levels where you have less time to make decisions, and the computer may become harder to beat.

  5. Winning & Losing:
    • After each round, you will see the result (Win, Lose or Tie)
    • The game ends if the computer reaches 7 points. At this point, the computer wins, and the game will stop.
    • You can try to win by increasing your score and advancing through the levels, but if the computer reaches 7 points, you will lose.

  6. Sounds:
    • Sound effects will play when you click a button, win, lose, or tie a round.

  7. Resetting the Game:
    • If the computer wins, you can reset the game by clicking the "Reset Game" button, which will restart the scores and levels back to the beginning.
